Wixonic Privacy Policy

Last updated Saturday, August 31, 2024, 14:10 UTC.

A. Personal Data We Collect B. Sharing of Personal Data C. Personal Data Retention D. Your Privacy Rights E. Specific Use Cases

We believe that your Personal Data should not be collected. That is why we only collect Personal Data necessary for the functionality and use of our Products, as well as the data that You have chosen to make public.
Please keep in mind that this policy only applies to Wixonic websites and products.

If you have any questions, please contact us.

A. Personal Data We Collect

Personal Data is collected from you directly, and from Third Parties. The personal data we collect depends on variables like how you interact with the Products we made, and the features you use. Below, we detail the information we collect:

From You
  • Account Data: We collect certain information when you open an account, such as display name, email address, password, IP address, or browser language.
  • User Generated Content: We collect certain information as part of the information you provide on our products, such as comments, biography, profile status, or pronouns.
  • Feedback and Support Data: When you seek customer support, we collect details like text or files you provided.

From Third Parties
  • Google Firebase: We are using services from Google Firebase (Firebase Hosting, Firebase Cloud Functions, Firebase Authentication, Firebase Firestore, Firebase Cloud Storage), see their privacy policy. When available, we chose to use europe-west1 servers, located in Belgium (more).

B. Sharing of Personal Data

We may share Personal Data with the following recipients:

  • Other Users and the Public: Depending on your account settings, we may share Personal Data with other users and the public. You control what information is made public. Keep in mind that you can always make your account private.

C. Data Retention

The Personal Data we collect is retained as long as your account is active. These data are necessary for the operation of our products and websites. When you decide to delete your account, all Personal Data associated with You will be permanently erased from our systems.

D. Your Privacy Rights

You have specific legal rights regarding your Personal Data:

  • Right of access by the data subject: we must provide You all your Personal Data we have when requested.
  • Right to rectification: we must help You rectify your Personal Data without undue delay when requested.
  • Right to erasure (Right to be forgotten): when requested, we must delete all your Personal Data without undue delay.

If You wish to exercise your rights, please contact us.

E. Specific Use Cases

In addition to the general provisions outlined above, this section addresses specific use cases where certain functionalities or services involve unique handling of Personal Data.

1. W.L.M.A. (Discord Application)

When You interact with our Discord Application, the following types of data may be collected:

  • Account Data: We collect certain information, such as your Discord ID, your Discord username and your Discord display name.
  • Provided Content: We collect certain information such as commands, command options and modals content you provided.
  • Public Content: We may collect publicly available text messages for moderation.

All Personal Data collected through the Discord Application is required for interaction. We do not share your Personal Data with third parties outside of the functionalities provided by the Application. We do not collect any Personal Data when You do not interact with the Discord Application.

For more details on how your Personal Data is managed in this context, please refer to the Discord Privacy Policy.

Our Discord Application complies with the Discord Developer Policy.
